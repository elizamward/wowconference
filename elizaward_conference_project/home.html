Join Us From May 21st-27th to Celebrate

WOW

Women on Web is a conference that focuses on highlighting the work of women in the web design field. This will be the conferences fifth annual meeting. This year, the conference is being held in Washington D.C., the United States capital. Previous locations for the conference have been London, San Fransisco, New York, and Seattle. In addition to our seven lovely female speakers that will be hosting events, there will be additional opportunities for interested members of the design world to get involved in the conference. Events are open to all ages and genders. We hope to see you all there!
